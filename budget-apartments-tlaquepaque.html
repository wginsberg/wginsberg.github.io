Shared Apartment in Tlaquepaque


Accommodation can also be booked without taking a Spanish Course!

Stay in a shared furnished apartment within a short walk of schooland the center of Tlaquepaque. The apartment accommodation option gives you more flexibility and privacy than a homestay.

At the shared apartments you will have your own private room but where you share the bathroom, kitchen and living room with fellow students.

Placement confirmation and contact details will be forwarded to you prior to your arrival. The Guadalajara Language Center shared apartment option can be selected on our online application form.
