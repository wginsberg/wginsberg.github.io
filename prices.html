Spanish Course Prices

We offer one of the most competitive pricing structures of all Spanish Language Schools in Mexico. Guadalajara Language Center gives you a variety of options when setting out on your course.

Our students appreciate the flexiblity of our programs. You can choose to learn in a group ( no more than 6 students ) - for a more interactive, group orientated learning experience and also of course a great way to meet new friends from all over the world. In our Group Program, you have the option to study 2 hours or 4 hours per day, Monday to Friday. Or, you can choose Private Tutoring, one-on-one with one of our teachers. We offer our private students an even greater flexibility in their program. You can make your own schedule during the week. These hours can be used when you want after consultation in advance with our Program Director.


Note: Unlike many other language schools, GLC does NOT charge the following:-


  • Subscription fees
  • Materials fees
  • Cancellation fees
  • Administration fees
