Activities



In addition to our Spanish classes we offer a variety of activities to complete your stay. On our website we only show a small overview of some of the activities that you can do. We offer many other activities and not all are mentioned here on the website including weekend trips with an overnight in for example Guanajuato, Mexico City or Puerto Vallarta.

Upon arrival you will receive a welcome package that includes maps of the city, brochures on local sites and interests and an activities calendar.

After classes on the first day you will receive a presentation on the week’s activities and events. At this time questions can be answered, trips scheduled and personalized group travel arranged.



For individual trips we have drivers and guides available. These private trips can be directly arranged with the guides. Prices vary for personalized trips.

The activities and/or a driver/guide can also be booked without taking Spanish classes.

If you have any questions about activities before your arrival please use our contact page or call us at our toll-free number from the USA or Canada, 1-866-275-5222.
